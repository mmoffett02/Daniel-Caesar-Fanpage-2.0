The Best R&B Artist of our Time: Daniel Caesar

Bio About Daniel Caesar

Daniel Caesar was born in Ontario, Canada, in 1996. His birth name is Ashton Dumar Norwill Simmonds. He was born from Bajan and Jamaican descent, into a family of four children. After he was kicked out at the age of 17, he started to pursue his music career. In 2015, Daniel's career took a turn. He started to gain a ton of recognition for his work. Ever since, he has been one of the most popular R&B artists in the United States.
